Upcoming Steam Events

Steam hosts a number of seasonal and thematic sales ("Fests") and events throughout the year. We hope you'll join us! Here's what's currently announced.

Seasonal Sales

Four times a year, we run Steam-wide seasonal sale events. Any game released at least 30 days before the event start date is welcome to participate with a discount. Enter and submit your discount for each via the Discount Management page.

Themed Sale Events (Fests)

These events spotlight a particular category of games with corresponding eligibility criteria; while discount games are prioritized, free games, games in Coming Soon, and games with demos are also welcome to participate. Each of these events will be promoted on the homepage of Steam and organized into sub-categories relevant for each event.

For more information, please see the Steam Themed Sale Events documentation.

Next Fest

Running three times a year, Steam Next Fest is a multi-day celebration where fans can try out demos, chat with developers, watch livestreams, and learn about upcoming games on Steam. For developers, Steam Next Fest is an opportunity to get early feedback from players and build an audience for a future launch on Steam. Upcoming (unreleased) games with a playable demo are eligible to participate.

For more information, please see the Steam Next Fest documentation.
